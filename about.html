Our Story

Founded in 2020 by Bhekumuzi Mahomane, Haya Ngizwe Events was born from a passion for empowering voices and creating platforms for artistic expression. We believe in the transformative power of art, especially spoken word poetry, to connect communities and inspire change.

Our mission is to deliver professional, high-impact events that are both memorable and meaningful. From intimate poetry slams to large-scale gala dinners and cultural festivals, we handle every detail with precision and creativity. We are more than just event planners; we are culture creators, artist managers, and digital experts dedicated to bringing your vision to life.

A Word From Our Founder

"We started Haya Ngizwe to build a home for the stories that need to be told. Every event is an opportunity to amplify a new voice and create a shared experience that lasts a lifetime."

- Bhekumuzi Mahomane
