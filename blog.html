Announcing: WOMAN IN POETRY: UNAPOLOGETICALLY HERSELF

Published on August 05, 2025

Haya Ngizwe Events is thrilled to announce our next major event, "WOMAN IN POETRY: UNAPOLOGETICALLY HERSELF." This special showcase, taking place on September 27, 2025, at The love of God disability centre, is dedicated to the powerful and diverse voices of women in the spoken word community. Prepare for an unforgettable day of raw emotion, profound storytelling, and fierce performances.
